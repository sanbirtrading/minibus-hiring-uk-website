ABC Travel - Wedding & Chauffeur Hire Services

ABC Travel wedding car service with experienced and friendly chauffeurs in the vehicle of your choice from a selection of luxury cars.

Our luxury cars including Mercedes S class, BMW 7 series, Bentleys, Rolls Royce, Mercedes V Class people carriers and Luxury VIP Mercedes 19 seater Mini Coach will ensure that your special day is one to remember.

Planning Your Wedding Day With ABC Travel

We will assist in the planning and use of your chosen chauffeur wedding car to bespoke our service to make your special day the event that you have always wanted.

It may be possible to use the car for several trips if timings allow, if you require vehicles to take guests to the reception venue or to book a car to take you to the Airport or hotel for your honeymoon we will offer a discount.

Any special requests with the vehicle we will be happy to oblige if at all possible, flowers, ribbons, music, etc.
